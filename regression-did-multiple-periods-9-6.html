Brief Practical Discussion

Still needs parallel trends
With more than two periods, it is easier to inspect trends: do treated and control groups look similar before the policy? The identification logic is the same, but we have richer information about dynamics.
Use: Stronger credibility when pre-trends are flat and post-trends diverge.
Rule of thumb: If possible, include at least one pre-period before the policy.
